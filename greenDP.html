Green Diamond Project

Green Diamond is a new frontier for creation of mass Career opportunities for Nigerians brighter future ahead of us especially through this Green Diamond beyond oil economy project because of the worldwide need for qualified practitioners to service the increasing consumer demand for natural health care.

There are various types of careers in Green Diamond that will solve the problems of the government in job creation:
helping people as clinicians,
supporting others in a multi-modality clinic,
working with manufacturers
conducting research and development,
technical writing,
working within hospitals and other health services.
