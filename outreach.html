Outreach
Our Mission is to save lives, empower people, improve and ensure better living conditions for the less privileged everywhere. Hence we created the Life Saver Rescue Mission Initiative as a means to carrying out this laudable objective.

Read on and see our archives to see how we have been illuminating lives for almost two decades

Objectives of Life Saver Rescue Mission

  • To build schools for the children of the less privileged.
  • To empower the youth through vocational training and skill acquisition programmes.
  • To establish clinics and health centres for the prevention and treatment of Malaria, TB and HIV/AIDS.
  • To network with other NGOs, FBOs, and CBOs, for cooperation in area of exchange programmes.
  • To partner with governments, agencies, corporate organizations and individuals to carry out low-cost mass housing projects.
  • To help provide the necessities of life such as food, clean water, clothing, shelter and healthcare to the needy especially the women and children.
  • To formulate remedies from Africa's rich medicinal plants and to use the same in preventing and treating Malaria, TB, HIV/AIDS and the common diseases.
  • To organize strategic health campaigns and seminars to help curb the scourge of Malaria, TB and HIV/AIDS in Africa particularly among rural communities and wipe the tears of those scarred by these raging pandemics.

Our Outreach Projects

Our outreach is grouped into subcategories for effectiveness.

Feed the Child Project

Every Sunday the Mission provides sumptuous meals for 80-100 children free of charge. Those who are diagnosed of any ailment among them are treated with M&G Life Saver Health Products. Feed the Child Project has been on since May 2006.

Community Health Day

A day in the month is set aside as Community Health Day. Issues about general health, sanitation and hygiene are discussed. Resource persons who must be qualified medical practitioners, nurses and nutritionists are invited to talk to the people on selected subjects.

Family Health Programme (FHP)

Family Health Programme (FHP) is A Bi-monthly family health programme for members of the public. FHP will focus on common family health problems and preferred solutions. The objective is to stem the rising tide of maternal and infant mortality rate.

Life Saver Africa School Project

Life Saver is currently working on a school construction project based in Abuja, Nigeria for underprivileged children in rural areas. The school is a Christian school where students will be fed relevant skills and knowledge that will enable them to provide for themselves and families. Land has been secured for the school project within the Kaura District where the displaced children are located.
